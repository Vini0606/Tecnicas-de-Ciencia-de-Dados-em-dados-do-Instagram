\begin{resumo}[ABSTRACT]
 \begin{otherlanguage*}{english}
This research project proposes the development and validation of a hybrid methodology to analyze comment data extracted from Instagram of Brazil’s governors. The approach integrates two Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques: sentiment analysis, to identify polarities and emotions; and topic modeling, to extract the central themes of the discussions. In addition, a clustering framework will be employed to group the posts by similar characteristics. The specific objectives include structuring a text processing pipeline, applying these techniques in an integrated manner, and validating the results. This research is expected to contribute with an innovative analytical model capable of providing a deeper and more multifaceted understanding of sentiment and topics in large volumes of unstructured social media data, demonstrating the advantages of a synergistic approach in opinion and market research.
  
\vspace{0.5cm}
\noindent
\textbf{Keywords}: Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, \textit{Machine Learning}.
  
 \end{otherlanguage*}
\end{resumo}
